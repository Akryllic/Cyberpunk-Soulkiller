## Performance - Everyone

### Q: How much VRAM do I need?
**A:** 16GB+ of VRAM is strongly recommended.

### Q: Should I enable ray tracing with this modlist?
**A:** Soulkiller is built around Ray-Tracing and Path-Tracing; User configuration is needed to properly enable the latter.

### Q: Why should I set the Shader Cache to 10GB?
**A:** A larger shader cache reduces compilation stutters and improves frame time consistency as the game compiles new shaders.

### Q: Does Soulkiller include Ultra+ / Do you recommend using Ultra+?
**A:** No - Ultra+ is mostly placebo, and where it legitmately changes something, it does so by significantly impacting stability

## Perfomance - AMD Users Only

### Q: Are there any steps I can take as an AMD GPU User?
**A:** Yes - You can enable Optiscaler

## Mods & Gameplay

### Q: How do I raise carry capacity? 
**A:** Under **Mod Settings**, locate **Flesh and Chrome - Carry Overhaul**, and adjust the carry capacity slider.

### Q: My Stamina drains when I have multiple weapons?!
**A:** By default you have 2.5 slots for weapons. Two-Handed Weapons use 1.0 slots, and One-Handed Weapons use 0.5 slots. You can increase the number of slots by equipping holsters or backpacks. 

### Q: Can I change recoil?
**A:** Under **Mod Settings**, locate **Flesh and Chrome**, and toggle off **Disable Recoil Recovery**. However, I encourage you to fix the core issue of having bad mechanics as this will improve your gaming experience for all shooters.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: Linux?
**A:** No.

### Q: My antivirus is interfering with installation. What do I do?
**A:** Add the modlist folder, game executable, and redmod executable to your antivirus exclusion list. You may also need to temporarily disable the antivirus during installation.
